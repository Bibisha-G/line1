Welcome to

The Blue Line Hospital


BKG group's Blue Line Hospital is considered as the most reputable healthcare facility in the nation. It is dedicated to providing patients with individualized and compassionate treatment while providing Quality Health Care in the Nation's Service. It is also praised for starting the private healthcare revolution in the nation. Blue Line is now regarded as one of Kathmandu, Nepal's premier hospitals. The hospital continues to provide full-service, multi-specialty treatment. It is one of the greatest critical care hospitals in the nation.

Since its founding in 1993, Blue Line has risen to prominence and become Nepal's top healthcare service provider, affecting the lives of more than 2 million people both domestically and overseas.



Why choose us?

Our Specialty


We attract top consultants and experts to work with us because of our reputation for providing excellent clinical care and a welcoming environment, as well as our ongoing investment in infrastructure and technology.

We collaborate with thousands of the most skilled experts and healthcare professionals from all across Nepal. Several of our consulting physicians are regarded as thought leaders in their fields on a global scale. Individuals can only work at Blue Line Hospital if they fulfill stringent requirements that are supervised by our Medical Advisory Committees.

Affordable Quality Care

Through its award-winning hospitals and clinics, Blue Line Hospital is dedicated to provide healthcare that is efficient, effective, and affordable.

Both individuals with insurance and those who choose to cover their own costs can receive care from us.

For those patients who self-fund or pay for their own care, we can offer:

  1. Services and treatments that are economical
  2. Secure and safe treatment
  3. Free, no-obligation prices are available for a variety of procedures, examinations, and consultations.
